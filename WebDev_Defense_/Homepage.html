ABOUT OUR WEBSITE

Philippines has numerous numbers of kidnapping or rape cases in the year 2022. A lot of victims were minor or women. We should always appreciate life since it is the most valuable gift anyone could ever get. We must defend our lives from those who wish to harm us if we are to appreciate them.
This website intends to assist the community in eliminating or reducing kidnapping incidents by educating them or raising awareness to those people by learning some suitable self-defense techniques that they can train in and utilize in dangerous situations


KIDNAPPING IN THE PHILIPPINES

The Philippine National Police (PNP) said on Thursday that kidnapping instances from January through July this year were largely connected to POGO (Philippine Offshore Gaming Operator), although most of these have already been settled. According to PNP spokesperson Police Colonel Jean Fajardo, "We have registered 25 occurrences of kidnapping episodes from January to July 2022, the majority of them were POGO-related."
